Healthy practices to fight Covid 19 Effectively

Based on the guidelines proposed by WHO

Maintain personal hygiene

Avoid touching surfaces, in public settings, in case people infected with COVID-19 have touched them. Clean surfaces regularly with standard disinfectants.

Vaccination

When it’s your turn, get vaccinated. Follow local guidance and recommendations about vaccination.

Social Distancing

Stay at least 1 metre away from others, even if they don’t appear to be sick, since people can have the virus without having symptoms.

Wear mask

Wear a well-fitting three-layer mask, especially when you can’t physically distance, or if you’re indoors.
